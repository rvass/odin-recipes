Amelia's Tuna Macaroni Salad

Description

This tuna macaroni salad will remind you of Grandma's. I love this when the weather is warm. One of the basic comfort foods!

Ingredients

  • 1 (12 ounce) package elbow macaroni
  • 1 (10 ounce) can baby peas, drained
  • 2 stalks celery, chopped
  • 1 (5 ounce) can tuna, drained
  • 1 cup mayonnaise
  • 2 tablespoons sweet pickle relish
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 3 large hard-cooked eggs, quartered
  • 1 pinch paprika, for garnish

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Cook elbow macaroni in boiling water, stirring occasionally, until tender yet firm to the bite, about 8 minutes. Drain and rinse under cold running water.
  2. Stir together macaroni, peas, celery, tuna, and onion in a large bowl. Mix in mayonnaise, relish, salt, and pepper. Garnish with egg wedges and a sprinkle of paprika. Cover and chill for at least 1 hour before serving.
